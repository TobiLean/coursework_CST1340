About us

Pine books

Pine books is the Largest Charitable Online and Instore Bookstore that caters towards providing affordable books; with a plethora of books to choose from! We don't only sell our books at relatively cheap rates to attract customers but we do so because we acknowledge the struggles of affording textbooks as a student and to also provide less privileged consumers with the opportunity of being able to immerse themselves in a world of pure imagination, educating themsleves and simply just exploring different things through reading.
